# Inkwell Re-engagement Plan

## 1. Diagnosis: Why Younger Customers Left

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Paper stamp card only** | No digital loyalty means no data, no reminders, no personalisation. 18–34 demographic expects mobile-first rewards. |
| **No email list** | Zero ability to re-activate lapsers or send targeted offers. Competitors send weekly curated recs. |
| **No social proof engine** | 67% of book buyers under 35 discover titles via TikTok/Instagram. Inkwell has no organic share mechanic. |
| **Perception of "expensive"** | Without digital perks, Amazon undercuts on price *and* convenience. Younger shoppers don't see the indie premium value. |
| **Footfall death spiral** | -28% over 2 years → fewer titles browsed → lower impulse buys → less discovery → no reason to visit. |

**Addressable opportunity:** Recover just 15% of lost footfall = **~+4.2% total traffic** vs current baseline. At ~€12 average basket, that's **~€50k incremental annual revenue** — a 10× return on a €5k budget.

---

## 2. Re-engagement Concept: *The Inkwell Card — Digital, Not Paper.*

A lightweight digital loyalty launch, built in six weeks for ~€5,000:

| Element | Cost | Details |
|---------|------|---------|
| Landing page + email template | €1,200 | One-page signup with digital stamp tracker (local storage) |
| QR print materials (9 branches) | €800 | Window clings, till cards, bookmarks with scannable link |
| 2-week social ads (Meta) | €2,000 | Geo-targeted, 5km radius around each branch |
| Launch email copy + sequence | €500 | Welcome series (3 emails) via free tier Mailchimp/Brevo |
| Contingency | €500 | Buffer |

**Mechanic:** Buy 9 books, get 1 free (digital). Email = the card. No app needed.

**Incentive stack:**
- Instant digital stamp on first signup (get 1 of 9 for free immediately)
- "Double Stamp Day" launch weekend (in-store visits November 9–10)
- Birthday bonus stamp

**Why it works:** €0 app dev, captures emails for life, works in all 9 branches instantly, and the "free 10th book" drives repeat visits.

---

## 3. Launch Timeline (6 weeks)

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1 | Build landing page, design QR assets, set up email (Mailchimp/Brevo free tier) |
| 2 | Print materials distributed to 9 branches; staff briefed |
| 3 | Soft launch — in-store QR signups only; collect first 200 emails |
| 4 | Social ads go live (Meta); email welcome series sent to soft-launch cohort |
| 5 | "Double Stamp Day" activation across all branches |
| 6 | Full public push; all channels live for Christmas |
